✦ Our Story

Trusted Travel Partner Since 2010

Andavar Tyres & Travels was established with a vision to provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation services in Kumbakonam and across Tamil Nadu. Located at 848, Karaikal Main Road, Muthupillaimandapam, Kumbakonam, we have been serving thousands of happy customers.

Starting with just a few vehicles, we have grown into one of the most trusted travel service providers in the region, with a fleet of over 50 vehicles ranging from compact sedans to luxury coaches.
